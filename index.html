Disclaimer & Legal Notice

How it works. All speech synthesis runs locally in your browser. No audio, text, or voice data is uploaded to any server. However, on first use (and after cache clears), the app downloads AI model weights (~2.5 GB) from Hugging Face (huggingface.co) and runtime libraries from jsDelivr (cdn.jsdelivr.net). These downloads expose your IP address, browser user-agent, and referrer to those services under their respective privacy policies. Once cached, no further network requests are made during synthesis.

AI-generated content. All audio produced by this tool is synthetic speech generated by an AI model (OmniVoice). Downloaded MP3 files contain machine-readable metadata (ID3v2) identifying them as AI-generated in accordance with EU AI Act Art. 50. Do not remove or alter this metadata.

User obligations under the EU AI Act. If you publish or distribute audio generated by this tool, you are legally required to disclose its synthetic nature to your audience (Art. 50(4)), including audible disclaimers where applicable. Using this tool to impersonate real individuals without their explicit consent, to create deceptive deepfakes, or to strip or circumvent AI content-marking is prohibited and may constitute a violation of the EU AI Act and other applicable law.
